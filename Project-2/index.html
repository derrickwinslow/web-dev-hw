Introduction


The Triangle Choke is a submission where the fighter turns a defensive position
into an offensive position! Utilizing timing and speed
the defensive fighter will capitalize on the offensive fighter's mistake.

Things Required

  1. Closed Guard
  2. I prefer an over-hook on either arm
  3. One grip on opponent's wrist
  4. Flexibility
  5. Speed
  6. Boa Constrictor-like leg squeeze
