Defensive Submission

Directions
Step Image Directions Details
Step 1 Isolate one arm by utilizing an overhook.
  • In this position, I am overhooking my opponent's right arm with my left.
  • Block other arm with hand to avoid strikes and keep legs locked.
Step 2 Once posture is controlled make your move
  • Notice how I am pusing my opponent's arm away from me.
  • Clear the opponent's arm from your leg
  • Look how my right leg is creating space to pass the arm
    1. I'm moving my right leg up towards opponent's neck
    2. Keep control of opponent's opposite arm
Step 3 Lock the submission up
  • Look how my right foot is under my left knee, this is important!
  • Use your left hand to grab left foot to hold submisison in place.
  • Tighten the choke bringing your left knee toward your body
Step 4 Choke is now locked, squeeze your legs together and pull down on head.
  • Make sure to bring opponent's trapped right arm across his neck.
  • Exhale then squeeze your legts and pull down on the head, pretend you're a Python.

Now that you know a defensive martial art position, walk in the night with confidence!
